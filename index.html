Fitness Connect

Fitness Connect is a fitness app that incorporates meal tracking , a database of workout programs and exercises to help users reach their fitness goals. To start I conducted a competitive analysis of the other fitness apps on the market drawing out their strengths and weaknesses followed by user interviews to find out their needs in a fitness app. With the research done I proceeded to map out user flows and sketch out wireframes and with revisions through user feedback I proceed with the final screens.
