About

My Story

My name is Jackie Li a UX/UI designer based in California. I am passionate about designing interfaces that are intuitive, easy to use and make an impact on people’s lives. I hold an Associate of Arts degree in graphic design from Golden west college and with that, I’ve been able to build my eye and discern the difference between good and bad design. This understanding has allowed me to see the impact a good design can make. I can see a project from start to finish with experience conducting user surveys, creating wireframes, mockups, and prototyping.
